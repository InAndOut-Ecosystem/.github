# Development Notice

The proposed solution offers no inherent business value for an enterprise, meaning it is unlikely to be integrated into existing business systems. Consequently, the necessary data for maintaining and operating the system will be unavailable.

Given the lack of utility, further development of the project's features will be halted. However, the **Minimum Viable Product (MVP) will be fully implemented** as it serves as a valuable learning experience regarding Service-Oriented Architecture (SOA).

The MVP includes:

- Providing a client interface for Customers
- Create the service responsible for solving the TSP problem
- Mock data for a quick demo
